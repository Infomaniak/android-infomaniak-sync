Infomaniak Sync enables you to synchronise your contacts and calendars between Infomaniak's WorkSpace and your Android devices.

Thanks to this app, you can view your contacts and calendars anywhere, directly on your Android devices.

Based on the Open Source DavDroid solution, this application guarantees the privacy of your personal data

To find out more about Infomaniak's WorkSpace: https://www.infomaniak.com/fr/outils-professionnels/workspace
